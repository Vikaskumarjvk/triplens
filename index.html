Why no real login?

Real accounts need a paid server that stores your data — which also makes whoever runs it responsible for protecting it (sensitive: it's about which cards you hold). LoungeLens stays free and private by keeping everything on your own device and letting YOU carry it with a code. Treat the code like the data itself: don't post it publicly.

About LoungeLens

A free, private tool to see which lounges your credit cards actually open in India.

⚠️ Not financial advice

LoungeLens helps you understand lounge access. It does not recommend taking on debt and is not affiliated with any bank, card issuer, DreamFolks, Priority Pass, or any lounge operator. Card and lounge rules in India change often. Always confirm the current terms with your bank and at the lounge desk before you rely on access. Apply for credit cards responsibly.

🔒 Your privacy

Everything you enter — your cards, your visits, your trips — is stored only in your own browser (localStorage). It is never sent to any server. There is no account, no login, no tracking, no analytics, no cookies. If you clear your browser data, it's gone. If you open the app on another device, it starts fresh. You can export your data to a file any time from the Data Health tab.

📊 Where the data comes from

The card and lounge information is compiled and confidence-tagged by the maintainer, last reviewed on the date shown in the footer. It is directionally accurate but not guaranteed current. Each entry carries a confidence badge and a "verify" pointer. The app ages its own confidence over time and flags what to re-check. Treat it as a smart starting point, not the final word.

💬 Found something wrong?

Lounge access changed? A card's benefit is different now? Log your real experience in the Lounges tab (got in / refused) — that feeds the verify queue and, if you export and share the file, helps everyone using the app. That's how the data stays honest without a server.
